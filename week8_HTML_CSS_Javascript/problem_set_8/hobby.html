Badminton

I started playing badminton at the age of 6 when i saw my idol Lee Chong Wei play to television.
For me, badminton is an amazing sport that challenges both mental and physical limits of
myself. It also allowed me to make many new friends along the way because I play
the sport every week with different friend groups.
